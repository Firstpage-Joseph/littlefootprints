Quality Preschools in Singapore with Infant Care and Childcare Programmes

Conveniently located in Singapore's heartland neighbourhoods, Little Footprints Preschool operates a vast network of SPARK- and POP-recognised childcare centres located islandwide, welcoming both local and international children into our vibrant, diverse community.

As one of the top preschools in Singapore, we are committed to providing quality childcare, infant care, nursery, and kindergarten programmes at affordable, subsidised fees. We provide a safe, supportive, and engaging environment where children from 2 months to 6 years old are nurtured into confident communicators, problem solvers and independent thinkers. Our early childhood curriculum is mapped to MOE and ECDA frameworks, ensuring that learning is developmentally appropriate, progressive, and holistic .

Our bilingual preschool environment in English and Chinese gives children a strong foundation for communication, literacy, and future learning. With caring educators and a curriculum that balances academics with character building, we prepare children to thrive in school and beyond.

The Little Footprints Experience

Wondering what to expect throughout your child’s preschool journey with us? As one of the best preschools in Singapore, we take pride in offering a quality educational experience that remains accessible through childcare programme subsidies and our Partner Operator (POP) status.

At Little Footprints Preschool, your child will not only learn key skills important for their school years ahead but also important life values, fostering communication, problem-solving skills and collaboration with others. This is made possible through our focus on experiential learning, character building and bilingual exposure through key themes and programmes.

All Set for School

Our curriculum equips children with both academic foundations and life skills, giving them the confidence and resilience to transition smoothly to primary school.

Immersive Bilingual Environment

Children are immersed in English and Chinese daily, with opportunities to listen, speak, read, and write in both. This bilingual approach strengthens communication skills early and prepares them for success in Singapore’s education system.

Community Engagement

Children actively participate in community initiatives such as Start Small Dream Big, Singapore Kindness Movement, and World Water Day. These experiences build empathy, social awareness, and civic-mindedness. 

Hands-on, Experiential Learning

Beyond the classroom, our children explore the world through active discovery and sensory exploration that brings abstract concepts to life. By engaging in tactile projects and outdoor excursions, they develop a natural curiosity and a deeper understanding of how things work in the real world.

Our Preschool Programmes

At Little Footprints Preschool, we believe every child deserves a head start. Our programmes are designed to foster a love for learning through a balanced mix of structured academics and creative play.

Infant Care (2–17 Months)

Our infant care programme provides a nurturing, secure, and sensory-rich environment for the youngest learners. Our dedicated educators focus on responsive caregiving, stimulating your infant’s physical, cognitive, and emotional growth through gentle interaction and purposeful play.

In accordance with ECDA's stringent safety and quality standards, we ensure every infant receives the personalised attention they need to reach their early developmental milestones with confidence.

Childcare (18 Months–6 Years)

From the first steps of a toddler to the confident strides of a preschooler, our childcare curriculum evolves with your child’s growing needs. We provide a vibrant setting where curiosity is encouraged and social skills are honed daily.

Playgroup (18 Months–2 Years): Multi-sensory activities build motor skills and early social interaction.

Pre Nursery and Nursery (3–4 Years): Hands-on language and numeracy discovery strengthens bilingual communication.

Kindergarten 1 and 2 (5–6 Years): School readiness focus ensures emotional and academic preparedness.

Our Recognition

SPARK-Certified Preschool

Little Footprints Preschool is assessed for the Singapore Preschool Accreditation Framework (SPARK) certification, conferred by the Early Childhood Development Agency (ECDA). Most of our centres are SPARK certified, reflecting our commitment to quality education and early learning.

ECDA Partner Operator Preschool

Little Footprints Preschool in Singapore is a Partner Operator (POP) appointed by ECDA. The POP scheme allows us to keep fees affordable at $680 for childcare and $1,290 for infant care. This ensures that children at our preschool receive quality education while also benefiting from improved career development opportunities for childcare professionals.
