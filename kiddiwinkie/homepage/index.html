KiddiWinkie Schoolhouse is a premium preschool in Singapore offering high-quality early education programmes grounded in neuroscience. As a trusted premium learning centre for preschool education, we develop confident, curious children with strong academic foundations and the resilience to thrive in Primary 1 and beyond.

About KiddiWinkie Schoolhouse

KiddiWinkie Schoolhouse accepts children from 2 months to 6 years old, offering infant care services (2–17 months) and preschool programmes from 18 months to 6 years old, including a playgroup programme and nursery classes. Our age-specific curriculum ensures each developmental stage receives appropriate guidance, care, and high-quality early education programmes tailored to school readiness.

KiddiWinkie offers a neuroscience-backed, inquiry-based preschool curriculum guided by our Natural Rhythm® pedagogy. As a premium learning centre for preschool education, we integrate purposeful play, authentic bilingual immersion in English and Chinese, and holistic development to prepare children confidently for Primary 1.

Parents looking for the best private childcare centre for toddlers in Singapore often prioritise strong teacher support, a safe environment, and a proven curriculum. KiddiWinkie Schoolhouse is widely trusted for its neuroscience-informed approach, purposeful play, bilingual immersion, and personalised care for children from 18 months onwards.
