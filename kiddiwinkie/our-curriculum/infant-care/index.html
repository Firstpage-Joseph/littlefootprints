Our Infant Care Programme

Learning at your child’s natural pace

Our private infant care services are thoughtfully designed around a research-backed curriculum, applied with sensitivity to the local Singapore context to support optimal brain development during the critical early years.

Sensory Oasis® for Infants

Infants need to tumble, play and learn. But just as important is a place to sleep, rest and recharge. That’s why every KiddiWinkie Schoolhouse is equipped with an exclusive Sensory Oasis® – a calming, multi-sensory environment designed to support your baby’s development from the earliest months.

Inspired by the proven Snoezelen concept, a controlled environment developed in the Netherlands in the late 1970s that blends gentle stimulation with a soothing atmosphere, soothing lights, gentle sounds, soft textures, and gentle interactive play come together to create a rich, multisensory experience that nurtures sensory development, supports emotional regulation and encourages curiosity in infants.

Progressing to Playgroup and Beyond

As infants grow, we ensure a smooth transition into the playgroup curriculum through our GROW Space, an extension of the Sensory Oasis® experience located within every classroom. This is a dedicated zone for early childhood emotional development, where our educators gently introduce age-appropriate strategies to help little ones manage their feelings and build resilience. This strong foundation prepares them not just for preschool, but for life.

Government Subsidies for Infant Care

If your child is a Singapore Citizen enrolled in a licensed infant care centre in Singapore, you may qualify for the following ECDA subsidies:

  • Basic Subsidy: Up to $600/month for infant care
  • Additional Subsidy: Up to $710/month for eligible working mothers
    • Applicable to families with gross monthly household income of $12,000 and below
    • Larger families (3 or more dependents) may apply under Per Capita Income (PCI) assessment

Permanent Residents may also be eligible for subsidies, subject to ECDA guidelines.

The Natural Rhythm® Pedagogy: Brain-Based Learning from Birth

In the first 17 months of life, a baby’s brain forms over 1 million neural connections every second. These early experiences lay the foundation for how a child thinks, feels and learns.

Unlike one-size-fits-all programmes, our infant care services incorporate our proprietary Natural Rhythm® approach, which provides children with a strong foundation for lifelong learning through individualised care.

Birth–6 months

Bonding and sensory security

6–12 months

Movement and active exploration

12–17 months

Early language and social awareness

Movement and Motor Development

Guided by the Natural Rhythm®, babies learn to move best when given the safety and freedom to explore. Through different types of play, infants strengthen gross motor skills, coordination and body awareness. 

Responsive Caregiving and Emotional Security

Our educators provide attentive, responsive care within the Early Childhood Development Agency (ECDA) recommendations. With consistent caregivers, babies are able to form secure attachments — an important foundation for developing self-regulation, emotional resilience, and a positive approach to learning.

Curiosity and Cognitive Growth

From birth, every sound, texture and sight is a learning opportunity. Sensory-rich experiences strengthen neural connections and stimulate healthy brain development.
