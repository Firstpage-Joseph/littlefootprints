Infant Care to Kindergarten in Jurong East


KiddiWinkie Jurong Gateway is a trusted infant care and preschool in Jurong East, offering programmes from 2 months to 6 years old — from infant care and pre-nursery to kindergarten. Conveniently located next to Jurong East MRT and Ng Teng Fong General Hospital, and just minutes from IMM and International Business Park, our centre is thoughtfully situated for families with children who live or work in the west.

As Singapore’s first preschool to be awarded the BCA Green Mark (Platinum) award, our Jurong Gateway centre brings together sustainable design and a holistic early childhood curriculum that evolves with your child at every stage. Whether your child is just beginning in infant care or preparing for kindergarten, we provide a nurturing environment that supports confident, joyful learning.

Contact us to arrange a visit or learn more about our infant care and preschool programmes.

How to Enrol Your Child at Our Jurong East Centre

Here’s what the enrolment process looks like from your first visit to your child’s first day.

1

Book a Centre Tour

Schedule a visit to see the classrooms, meet our educators, and experience the learning environment in-person.

2

Discuss Your Child’s Needs

Share your child’s age, routine, and developmental needs so we can guide you on the most suitable programme.

3

Check Availability

Our team will confirm current availability and discuss suitable start dates based on your preferred programme.

4

Submit Application

Complete the registration form and provide the required documents to secure your child’s place.

5

Settling-In Period

Your child will begin with a gradual settling-in process to help them feel safe, comfortable, and confident in their new environment.

Spaces at our Jurong East centre are limited, so we encourage early registration to avoid disappointment.
